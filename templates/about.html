Purpose of Verity

Spam is a common thing in today's society, this is focusing more on email spam detection. The purpose of Verity is to allow users to filter out spam that can be potentially harmful. If the email is considered spam, the program will tell the user if the entered email address is spam or not. The format will be email is spam/not spam. The main target audience of Verity will be anyone who has an email address.
